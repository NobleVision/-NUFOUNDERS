# NuFounders - Google Veo 3.1 Video Prompts

## Overview

This document contains 50 professionally crafted video prompts designed for Google Veo 3.1 to generate high-quality, cinematic videos for the NuFounders landing page. Each prompt features professional, fit, attractive Black women in empowering business and entrepreneurship scenarios.

**Technical Specifications:**
- Resolution: 1080p or 4K
- Duration: 5-15 seconds per clip
- Style: Cinematic, professional, aspirational
- Lighting: Natural, warm, professional studio or golden hour
- Color grading: Warm tones, high contrast, modern aesthetic

**For Course/Learning Sections:**

## Technical Notes for Veo 3.1

1. **Lighting Keywords:** Include "cinematic lighting," "golden hour," "natural light," or "professional studio lighting" for best results

2. **Camera Movement:** Specify "tracking shot," "slow motion," "shallow depth of field," or "steady cam" for dynamic footage

3. **Color Grading:** Request "warm color grading," "high contrast," or "modern aesthetic" for consistent look

4. **Subject Details:** Always specify age range, attire, setting, and emotional tone for accurate generation

5. **Duration:** Veo 3.1 works best with 5-15 second clips; longer narratives should be broken into segments
