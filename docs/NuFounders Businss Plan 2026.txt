AI-Native Upskilling & Business Formation Platform for Displaced Workers  

- An AI-Native platform supplemented by human expert instructors,  

- Up 10 to 25 curated upskilling training courses  
  - dynamically generated by AI subject-matter agents (SMAs),  
  - aided and iteratively prompted by Human Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)  

- free linkages to courses for monetizing income streams for end user  
  - A generic business formation, business planning and expansion platform  
    - helps end-users match selected upskilling courses with  
    - plausible entrepreneurship monetization modules/curriculums  

- Creating social and professional networking communities of displaced worker sub-groups of like-minded end-users,  
  - incorporating virtual events and  
  - in-person events  

- A proprietary, rolling, displaced workers.database tdocumenting the  
  - demographics,  
  - experiences,  
  - progress and  
  - unmet needs  

- Scholarships from sponsors and corporations seeking to hire for difficult-to-fill positions  

- Access to capital for aspirational end users willing to compete for debt and equity capital in order to monetize career goals,  
  - NUFOUNDER Quarterly pitch competitions
